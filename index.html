// pick one

Six tracks. Every team picks one.

Stretch ideas that span two tracks are fine — declare the primary track for judging.

Track 01

Customer-facing AI

Anything an end customer would directly see or talk to. Strong fit for design-heavy work and UX polish. Synthetic data only.

  • Conversational onboarding that replaces long forms
  • AI doc-upload coach that critiques photo quality live
  • In-app advisor in plain Spanish, 4–5 questions
  • Voice-first balance check / payment confirmation
Track 02

Sales & origination ops

AI that makes sales and credit teams dramatically faster. Strong fit for teams comfortable with messy real-world inputs.

  • One-page underwriting summary from the full doc set
  • KYC pre-check copilot — what's missing before they leave
  • Lead-scoring agent from intake transcripts
  • Voice-to-CRM: structured notes, follow-ups, tags
Track 03

Internal productivity copilots

AI tools for Nexu employees doing day-to-day work. Lowest data-sensitivity track — great fit for teams that want to ship something usable fast.

  • Internal docs Q&A with citations
  • Meeting summarizer with action items + owners
  • Auto-generated weekly team digest
  • Doc drafter for RFCs, post-mortems, project briefs
Track 04

Risk, credit & fraud

The heart of Nexu's business model. Highest-impact and highest-skill. Strong fit for data + AI + BI talent. Synthetic underwriting data only.

  • Fraud-pattern detector across application clusters
  • Anomaly watchdog on portfolio metrics
  • Document-tampering detector for IDs and pay stubs
  • Early-warning model for delinquency + draft outreach
Track 05

Data & BI automation

Make the data team's outputs faster, richer, accessible. Remove the bottleneck where the rest of the company waits for data.

  • Natural-language SQL with chart + visible query
  • Auto-narrated dashboards — what changed and why
  • Metric anomaly explainer with hypotheses
  • “Data dictionary” agent answering provenance questions
Track 06

Design & content AI

How Nexu creates, communicates, tests its product and brand. Strong fit for designer- and marketer-led teams.

  • Marketing-asset pipeline from a single brief
  • UX-research synthesizer over 20 interview transcripts
  • Design-system copilot respecting tokens
  • Brand-consistency checker on drafts and screens
